Welcome to RateMyBop.com!

Here, you can listen to said bops, rate them, and then play your favorite! Click the button to play a random song. Note that they may take a while to load in!

Once playing, rate the song with an integer value from 0 to 3. Any decimals will be rounded down.

After you have rated as many songs as you wish, click the button to play your favorite one! If you have multiple favorites, a random one will be selected.

We hope you enjoy using RateMyBop.com!
